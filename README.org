* SRFI-26 in CL: Notation for Specializing Parameters without Currying

#+BEGIN_SRC lisp
(mapcar (cut #'list 1 2 <> 3)
        '(1 2 3 4))
;=> ((1 2 1 3) (1 2 2 3) (1 2 3 3) (1 2 4 3))

(let ((list #'list*))
  (mapcar (cut list 1 2 <> 3)
          '(1 2 3 4)))
;=> ((1 2 1 . 3) (1 2 2 . 3) (1 2 3 . 3) (1 2 4 . 3))
#+END_SRC
